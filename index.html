FUTURE SKILLS SHORT COURSE

DevOps

Enrol now Download a brochure

Drive faster and more reliable software build and release cycles with DevOps. Learn the skills, tools, and processes to deliver business benefit and map out an implementation strategy for DevOps within your team.

100% online, mentor supported, workplace ready

Development and Operations (DevOps) is a set of tools and practices that automates processes helping build, test, and release software faster and more reliably. Walk away with a DevOps strategy that can be implemented in the workplace. Delivered completely online with Australia's leading DevOps consultancy Thoughtworks, have the confidence that the content you're learning is the industry's best.

Find out more in our FAQ section.

  • Define and discuss the key concepts and principles of DevOps
  • Comprehend and articulate business benefits of DevOps and continuous delivery
  • Explain the concepts of test automation, infrastructure automation, and build and deployment automation and apply them in your team
  • Critically evaluate how DevOps relates and benefits Lean and Agile methodologies.
  • Explore case studies of IT organisations transforming to adaptive IT and DevOps models.
  • Discuss the critical success factors for DevOps implementation
  • Analyse, critique, and recommend DevOps models and workflow within an organisational context
  • Appraise and select an appropriate strategy for continuous improvement including automation

Find out more in our FAQ section.

This course helps mid-senior level managers, technical staff, or those with a less technical background learn the foundational concepts, tools, and knowledge of how DevOps can assist your company digitally transform.​ ​

By the end of this course, you'll complete a DevOps implementation strategy for your business, and be prepped for an optional DevOps certification with our industry partner, the DevOps Agile Skills Association (DASA).
